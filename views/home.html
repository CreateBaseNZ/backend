How it works

Learn with

Projects

Projects guide students through the process of creating a fully realised, tested, and evaluated solution to a real-world problem by applying our 7-step creation process.

Teach with

Lesson plans

Each Project comes with a fully detailed series of lesson plans, providing a complete framework for teachers of every experience level while ensuring full curriculum alignment.

Create with

Simulations

Each Project lets students build, program and explore realistic solutions within a simulated environment. Students see their solutions come to life and, with flow-based programming, get to create robotic systems that they would never have access to in the real world.

One integrated Ecosystem

All of our offerings are delivered via one integrated experience. Story content and a virtual world makes it more fun than ever to learn how to create!
