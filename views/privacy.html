Privacy Policy

What we collect

As a student using our platform, we collect personal information from you, including information about your:

  • First and last names during sign up
  • Email address during sign up
  • The name of your school when joining a school group
  • The name of your class when you join or a teacher adds you to a class in your school group
  • Progress (for example, recording which tasks you have completed in a Project)
  • Performance (for example, recording your answers to tasks completed in a Project)
  • Engagement (for example, usage statistics like when you log in and how much time you spend on the platform)
  • Device (for example, your internet (IP) address, your location and the type of browser that you use, and its identity in the form of a "cookie")
  • Usage (for example, the content and features that you interact with the most)
  • Billing or purchasing information if you purchase an individual license

As a teacher using our platform, we collect personal information from you, including information about your:

  • First and last names during sign up
  • Email address during sign up
  • Information about your school, including the name, when you join or create a school group
  • The name of your class when you join or create a class in your school group
  • Progress (for example, recording which tasks you have completed in a Project)
  • Performance (for example, recording your answers to tasks completed in a Project)
  • Engagement (for example, usage statistics like when you log in and how much time you spend on the platform)
  • Device (for example, your internet (IP) address, your location and the type of browser that you use, and its identity in the form of a "cookie")
  • Usage (for example, the content and features that you interact with the most)
  • Billing or purchasing information if you purchase an individual licence or a license on behalf of your school

As any user of our website, we collect personal information from you, including information about your:

  • Email address if you sign up to our mailing list

What it's used for

We only collect your personal information in order to either process transactions, personalise the user experience, enable certain features, evaluate business performance or understand our customer segments. See below for more information about what we use specific personal information for.


As a student using our platform, we collect your:

  • First and last names so that you, your teacher(s) and your peers can identify your account
  • Email address so that you can recover your account
  • School name so that we can pair you with the correct school
  • Class name so that we can pair you with the correct class
  • Progress so that your teacher(s) can track what work you are completing and so that you can resume where you left off
  • Performance so that your teacher(s) can track how well you are completing your work
  • Engagement so that your teacher(s) can track how engaged you are with your work and the platform
  • Device information so that we can optimise the content that we display to you
  • Usage to help us develop and improve our platform
  • Billing or purchasing information if you purchase an individual license so that we can process your transaction

As a teacher using our platform, we collect your:

  • First and last names so that you and your peers can identify your account
  • Email address so that you can recover your account
  • School information so that we can confirm that you are a member of the school that you are trying to join or create a group for
  • Class name so that we can create a class for you inside your school group
  • Progress so that you can track what work you are completing and resume where you left off
  • Performance so that you can compare with your students
  • Engagement so that you can track your platform usage
  • Device information so that we can optimise the content that we display to you
  • Usage to help us develop and improve our platform
  • Billing or purchasing information if you purchase an individual licence or a license on behalf of your school so that we can process your transaction

Cookies

To collect some of the data described above, our platform may use cookies. A cookie is a small piece of information that is sent to your browser and stored on your computer’s hard drive, mobile phone or other devices. Cookies allow us to recognise our website users, although any user may disable the setting of cookies using a corresponding setting in their internet browser. Additionally, already set cookies may be deleted at any time via an Internet browser or other software programs. Unfortunately, some of our services may not function properly if your cookies are disabled, as cookies are necessary for the performance of some of our features.

External links

Our platform might contain links to external websites. Although we would never intentionally link to an external website that contains malicious content or poses a risk to data security, it is important to note that we have no control over these external parties. Please be careful to protect your privacy when visiting these websites.

Who we share with

We do not share your personal information with anyone other than in the rare circumstances where we are required to do so by law or to protect ourselves from criminals or fraudsters. We do not disclose personal information for business purposes to other third parties. We do not sell personal information. We will endeavour to keep your personal information safe.

Opting out

Providing some information is optional. If you choose not to:

  • Enter a first name, last name and email address you’ll be unable to create an account on our platform. You can, however, continue as a guest to access limited content
  • Enter billing information we'll be unable to process orders and transactions
  • Provide accurate information about your school you’ll be unable to create a school group
  • Enter an email address on our website we'll be unable to send you email notifications, and newsletters

If you do not think that we are doing a satisfactory job with your information, then please contact us with your concerns and we will determine what actions need to be taken. See below for more information about how to contact us for any inquiry.

Additional info

We keep your information safe by having security protocols for creating, reading, updating and deleting data and only allowing staff to access the data. You have the right to ask for a copy of any personal information we hold about you, ask for it to be corrected if you think it is wrong, or ask us to remove it from our data records. If you’d like to ask for a copy of your information or have it corrected or removed, please contact us at admin@createbase.co.nz, or +64 211 543 805, or 24 York Street Parnell, Auckland, 1052, New Zealand.
